What is a natural disaster?

Natural disaster is an unfavorable event that hinders human activity. It is a natural event with atmospheric, geological, or hydrological origins. Natural disasters constitute a major threat to health, safety, and property. They can occur at any time, without any warning, and can cause massive damages to the economy. Natural disasters are dangerous and are known to cause thousands of deaths each year, around the world. There are many kinds of natural disasters, such as floods, storms, earthquakes, and wildfires.
