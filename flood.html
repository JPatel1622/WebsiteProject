What is a flood?

A flood is an overflow of water in an otherwise dry area. There are many reasons for a flood, such as heavy rain, overflow of rivers, broken dams, etc. Regardless of the cause, floods can have devastating effects on property and people. In the U.S., floods cause more economic damage, destruction to property, and deaths than any other natural disaster.

How to prepare for a flood?

To stay safe during a flood, plan ahead. Keep your home safe by turning off the power, gas, and secure unstable building materials. Always be prepared to evacuate. Ensure that your car has enough gas, prepare a kit with food, first aid kit, etc., and listen to the radio for weather updates. ONLY take essentials with you.
